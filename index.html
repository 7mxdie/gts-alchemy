What is The Alchemist's Ledger?
The Alchemist's Ledger is a companion database for Gate to Sovngarde. It ranks the strongest 2- and 3-ingredient alchemy recipes by Potency, drawing on thousands of possible combinations, so you can see which are worth brewing before you spend your ingredients on them. It doesn't replace your alchemy table — it tells you what to aim for once you're there, and lets you browse every reagent and its effects along the way.
What is Potency?

Potency is a consistent ranking used to compare recipes.

It allows every valid combination to be measured using the same calculation, making it possible to sort recipes from strongest to weakest.

baseCost × (magnitude × duration ÷ 10)1.1

Potency is not your character's final in-game potion strength.

Final in-game strength is affected by:
Potency × Alchemy skill × perks × Fortify Alchemy gear × traits × other gameplay modifiers

Conceptual only — not a calculation the Ledger performs.

Your actual potions will still be affected by your Alchemy skill, perks, enchantments and any other gameplay modifiers.

Think of Potency as a way of comparing recipes fairly rather than predicting your exact crafting results.

Why are some recipes 2 ingredients and some 3?
A potion only gains an effect if at least two of its ingredients share it. Sometimes the strongest version of an effect comes from just two well-matched reagents; other times a third ingredient brings in an additional shared effect and produces something more powerful overall. The Ledger checks both and shows you whichever combination actually ranks higher — so you're never stuck guessing whether a third ingredient would have helped.
GTS vs GTS CE

GTS and GTS CE each come from their own export of Gate to Sovngarde's ingredient data — they aren't just different filters on the same list.

Gate to Sovngarde: Community Edition changes some existing ingredient effects and recipes, as well as adding new ones. Because the Ledger analyses that exported game data directly, switching between GTS and GTS CE automatically reflects those gameplay differences in the rankings.

Creation Club and additional mod-added ingredients can then be included or excluded using the filters inside the Ledger, on top of whichever collection you've chosen.

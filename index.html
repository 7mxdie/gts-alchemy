The reckoning

Each potion takes every effect shared by two or more of its reagents — matched by the underlying magic-effect record, so two effects that merely share a name don't wrongly combine. For a shared effect the strongest reagent wins. Base Potency scores each effect straight from its exported strength — baseCost × (mag × dur⁄10)^1.1, summed — with no player scaling; this is the reliable default ranking. Player Estimate layers on Alchemy skill, the Alchemist perk and Fortify Alchemy gear. Farmable only hides unique/limited reagents; Creation Club and Mod content drop ingredients you don't run. To stay fast at 400+ reagents, the ledger ranks combinations among the highest-potency reagents.

Known calculation caveats. GTS reworks the Alchemy perk tree, so Player Estimate perk scaling is not fully modelled yet (vanilla-style fallback). Fortify Alchemy behaviour under GTS is likewise not fully modelled. GTS's dynamic crafting — enhanced / impure potions — isn't modelled at all. And GTS applies a global value reduction that may live in a game setting rather than the records. Treat all numbers as a ranking / estimate, not exact merchant sale prices — Base Potency is the dependable ordering.
